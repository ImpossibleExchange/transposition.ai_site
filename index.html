What is transposition.ai?

Transposition.ai aims to become a one stop shop for all your local AI needs. 
It was thought up, because its creator had a .ai domain, interest in the area, and loads of free time. 
We hope this can foster a community of people to share information, experience, and maybe even collaborate together on projects.
